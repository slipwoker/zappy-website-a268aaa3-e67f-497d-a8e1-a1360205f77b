Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about shopping the NOVARO sale.

01

How long will sale items be available?

Our seasonal sale runs while stocks last. Once an item sells out, it is typically not restocked at the discounted price. We recommend checking back regularly as new markdowns are added throughout the sale period.

02

What is your exchange and return policy for sale items?

Sale items can be exchanged within 14 days of delivery. Returns for monetary refund are not available on sale products — instead, we offer store credit valid for 12 months. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and have all original tags attached.

03

Are sale items the same quality as full-price pieces?

Absolutely. Every NOVARO piece — whether full-price or marked down — meets the same rigorous quality standards. Sale pricing reflects seasonal rotation, not defects. Each garment is inspected before shipping to ensure it arrives in perfect condition.

04

How can I reach customer support?

Our support team is available via WhatsApp at 054-891-9881 or by email at . We respond to all inquiries within a few hours during business days. For order-related questions, please have your order number ready.

05

When does the next sale drop start?

We announce new sale drops exclusively through our newsletter and Instagram channel. Sign up at the bottom of the page to receive early access notifications. Major seasonal sales typically launch at the end of winter and summer collections.
